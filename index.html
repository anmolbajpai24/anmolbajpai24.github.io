About Me

I am a Game Developer who develops games using Unity and Unreal Engine. My vision is to make story driven games with a plot for exploration that deliver an enriching experience to the player with a focus on smooth gameplay, mechanics and well written code. It started out as an extensive interest in games, to trying to make them and then eventually loving the process of making them.

I have a good understanding of making 2D and 3D games using C# language in Unity. I'm working on a personal project which is a survival and exploration game titled 'Anvesion'.

I have worked in Caravel Tech, as an SDE intern in their newly formed gaming vertical CaravelGG that makes hypercasual games for android and publishes them on the play store and now I'm working on a personal project which is a survival game using Unreal Engine and blueprints!
