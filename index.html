About Me

I am a Software Developer passionate about creating immersive, story-driven games that offer players an enriching experience through engaging gameplay, refined mechanics, and clean, well-structured code. What began as a deep interest in playing games evolved into a passion for building them — and now, I truly enjoy the entire process of bringing interactive worlds to life.

I specialize in building 2D and 3D games using Unity (C#) and have strong expertise in UI development using Unity’s UI Toolkit. I have hands-on experience designing and implementing complete UI systems, crafting smooth player experiences, and developing gameplay systems from scratch.

I am currently working at Petals Studio, where I focus on building polished and responsive game UI using Unity’s UI Toolkit. Alongside this, I am also working on a personal survival and exploration game titled Anvesion, which reflects my vision of building story-rich games focused on exploration and player engagement.
